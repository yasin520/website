How to Play

Use your left thumb on the left joystick and your right thumb on the right joystick. Move both together to guide the ball upward through the playing field.

Avoid all black holes on the field. Collect yellow coins along the way to earn currency for the shop. Reach the green zone at the top to complete the level.

As levels progress, wind will push your ball in unexpected directions and moving obstacles will block safe paths. Stay calm, coordinate your hands, and adapt.

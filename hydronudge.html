Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Kido Studio operates Hydronudge. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

To provide hydration tracking and coaching features, we may collect the following:

  • Account information (email address) if you choose to create an account
  • Hydration logs you manually enter (amounts and timestamps)
  • App preferences and notification settings
  • Anonymous analytics to improve app performance

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered hydration coaching personalized to your routine
  • To sync your data across devices (if account is created)
  • To send smart reminders and notifications
  • To enable social features like leaderboards and friend challenges

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Health or medical records beyond what you manually log
  • Location data
  • Contacts or device access
  • Payment information (processed by App Store or Google Play)

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption via Firebase (Google), which complies with GDPR and other privacy regulations. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app.

Children's Privacy

Hydronudge is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
